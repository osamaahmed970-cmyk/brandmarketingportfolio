01 — Background

A New Value Tier for a Growing Franchise

Boardwalk Burgers identified a gap in its menu — customers wanted quality at an accessible price point. The answer was Boardwalk Select: a curated value menu tier designed to drive frequency and capture price-conscious customers without cheapening the brand.

The launch required a campaign that felt authentic, not discounted. Rather than running generic promotional ads, the strategy leaned into the creator community — letting real customers tell the story through UGC content, then amplifying the best-performing pieces as paid ads.

Strategic Decision
Instead of leading with branded creative, we launched with creator content as the primary paid asset — a UGC-first strategy that kept the campaign feeling organic while scaling reach through paid distribution.
02 — Campaign Creative

The Creative Suite

The campaign ran across 3 Meta campaigns with distinct creative strategies. A hero KV anchored the brand identity, supported by lifestyle content and UGC from the Boardwalk Select Squad. Each asset was A/B tested against the others to identify what resonated most with local audiences.

03 — Creator Program

Boardwalk Select Squad — UGC to Paid Pipeline

The Boardwalk Select Squad is an ongoing creator program built to generate authentic UGC from real customers — food creators, lifestyle influencers, and local community figures. Creators visited locations, filmed their experience, and posted organically.

The best-performing organic posts were then whitelisted and boosted as paid ads — a UGC-to-paid pipeline that kept costs low and authenticity high. Creator content consistently delivered CA$0.14 CPC vs CA$0.29 for branded creative — a 2x efficiency advantage.

05 — Key Learnings

What This Campaign Proved

✅
UGC Beats Branded on Cost Efficiency
Creator content delivered CA$0.14 CPC vs CA$0.29 for branded creative — a consistent 2x efficiency advantage. For value-focused campaigns, authentic content outperforms polished brand assets every time.
✅
Whitelisting Amplifies Organic Success
Running paid media through creator accounts rather than the brand page preserved the authentic feel of the content while unlocking paid reach. The result was high performance at low cost.
💡
Build a Standing Creator Roster
The Select Squad model — an always-on pool of creators generating content — is more scalable and cost-effective than one-off influencer activations. Relationships compound over time.
💡
Test UGC Across More Campaign Types
UGC was used for engagement and traffic campaigns. Given the efficiency advantage, testing creator content in conversion and lead gen objectives could yield significant results.
